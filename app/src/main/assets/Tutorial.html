Motif Learning Guidance

How to play

Welcom to Motif learning application!

When you first open the application, you must select a level first from the level list. And you can also choose the difficulty for each level from the settings page.

Easy Normal Hard
around 20 edges around 50 edges around 100 edges

After choosing the level and difficulty, you can begin to play the game!

There will be some nodes which represent DNA motifs. Your mission is to find out all the possible association between them. You can connect the nodes by touching the edges between them. Its color will be green if the association exits. Otherwise, it will be red. If you don't know which one to choose, you can press the hint button for help.

Just enjoy it!

Questions

Can I reset the data?

Yes, you can reset the data through "Reset All Progress" option in Setting page.

Why am I not able to see the nodes and edges after choosing the level?

The nodes and edges maybe are not in the center of the screen, so you can drag to see whether they are in the corner.
